About Us

Valumetrics uses machine learning to summarize 10k annual reports. We want to allow investors to easily understand 10ks and quickly get information on a company. Annual reports are highly regulated and hence, are way longer than they should be, in addition, we want to allow investors to get the necessary information quickly since it is very hard for investors to acquire valuable information from 10Ks as they are full of jargon and are extremely lengthy.

Valumetrics uses a natural language processing tool designed to summarize annual reports. We are aiming to provide investors with the essential information needed to make more informed decisions

Our Product

We have fine-tuned a state-of-the-art NLP model built by Google on Annual reports using gold-standard summaries made by the team. We have nine different models for the different sections of the annual report.

We also found that natural processing models like ChatGPT will not provide great results as they are designed to be general purpose NLPs. Our model incorporates training data that has been specifically built to summarize annual reports and should theoretically provide much higher Rouge scores than any other model on the market.
