About Us

Our goal is to create a state of the art NLP model for summarising annual reports. The models will all be trained on gold standard summaries that will make it much more efficient at summarising annual reports. The reason we feel this is needed is because there are a number of problems with annual reports right now and the way users use them.

The Problem

The first problem is that they are long and full of jargon as a result of regulation with there being a positive correlaton between time and the average lengths of annual reports. This makes it much more difficult for annual report users to find the relevant information quickly to make informed decisions.

Secondly, annual reports are tailored to investors, when in actuality a wide range of people access them. 31.5% of people who access annual reports are part of the core financial community. Our summary model will allow any user to interpret an annual report quickly and efficiently.

Finally, people only care about certain aspects such as ESG/sustainability which is not a heading in a 10k. The top three reasons an annual report is accessed is for reasons of financial performance, strategy and sustainability where information about may not be available in one place and no defined section in the traditional 10k.

Our Product

We want to reduce the information gap with a user centric, one-size fits all approach to accessing annual reports

We have fine-tuned a state-of-the-art NLP model built by Google on annual reports using gold-standard summaries made by the team. We have nine different models for the different sections of the annual report.

We also found that natural processing models like ChatGPT will not provide great results as they are designed to be general purpose NLPs. Our model incorporates training data that has been specifically built to summarize annual reports and should theoretically provide much higher Rouge scores than any other model on the market.
